# Product Vision

This app is a calm wellness companion.

It quietly helps users:
- avoid long sedentary periods
- drink water
- stretch
- breathe
- care for their body gently

The app should never feel:
- commercial
- stressful
- productivity obsessed
- noisy

---

# Emotional Goal

The app should feel like:
"a quiet peaceful presence beside the user."

---

# Core Features

- sedentary reminder
- hydration reminder
- stretch guidance
- breathing exercise
- daily wellness stats
- widgets
- desktop/mobile sync

---

# Product Principles

Always prefer:
- simplicity
- calmness
- clarity
- softness

Avoid:
- feature overload
- complicated onboarding
- account systems
- unnecessary notifications

---

# MVP Goal

Build the smallest beautiful version first.

Do not over-engineer.
